Performance Methodology

Performance Methodology

Returns are time-weighted, net of all management and performance fees, calculated monthly using industry-standard methodology. Portfolio performance shows representative managed accounts following Glenorchy Capital's investment strategies since inception on September 18, 2019. Returns assume reinvestment of all dividends and interest. Performance is historic and does not guarantee future results.

Benchmark & Comparison

Asymmetric Strategy: ACWI (iShares MSCI ACWI ETF) represents global equity market performance across developed and emerging markets

Dividend Strategy: DVY (iShares Select Dividend ETF) represents high-dividend equity performance in the U.S. market

Benchmark returns are also net of fees. Excess returns calculated as arithmetic difference between portfolio and benchmark returns.

Important Risk Disclosures

Past performance does not guarantee future results. Investment returns and principal value will fluctuate. Representative accounts may not reflect the experience of all clients due to differences in timing, market conditions, and individual circumstances. The Asymmetric strategy involves concentrated positions in undervalued sectors globally, which may result in higher volatility than diversified strategies. The Dividend strategy focuses on income-generating equities which may have different risk characteristics. International investing involves currency risk, political risk, and different regulatory environments.

Fee Structure

Returns shown are net of management fees (2% annually) and performance fees (20% above hurdle). Detailed fee disclosure available on our Fees page.
