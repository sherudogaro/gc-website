Glenorchy Capital Performance Disclosure

Last updated: January 2025

Important Investment Performance Notice

This page contains important disclosures regarding investment performance, risks, and regulatory requirements for Glenorchy Capital Ltd. as a registered investment advisor. Please read carefully before making any investment decisions.

Portfolio Definitions and Measurement Periods

Performance results are presented for two managed portfolios:

Asymmetric Capital Gains Portfolio (inception: September 2019): Seeks superior returns by investing in deep value global stocks within sectors expected to benefit from renewed investment.

Diversified Income Portfolio ("Dividend Portfolio", inception: May 2021): Designed to provide reliable income, replacing traditional bonds with diversified, global dividend-paying stocks.

Our performance figures are based on the first client account managed, selected for its comprehensive record since the inception of our managed accounts service. Time weighted return (TWR) data is calculated using a hypothetical starting amount of $500,000, compounding all results, and assumes no additions or withdrawals over the measurement period.

Calculation Methodology

Reported performance is calculated on a time weighted return basis (TWR) using account-level data exported directly from IBKR Portfolio Analyst. Performance assumes a constant portfolio value, with all cash flows and distributions reinvested in the portfolio. No money was added or withdrawn during the measurement period.

Fee Reporting and Adjustments

Gross returns are based on IBKR's default TWR export, which does not deduct any management or performance fees.

Net returns are adjusted to reflect the highest fee schedule Glenorchy Capital may charge (e.g., 2% management fee, performance fee as applicable). Fees are allocated pro rata over each year and subtracted from the gross returns on a monthly basis. This method ensures conservative, realistic net-of-fee figures for investor consideration.

Data Source and Verification

Performance statistics are derived from IBKR Portfolio Analyst, with underlying client account data and export logs held on file. Data provided has not been independently audited or verified and is prepared internally by Glenorchy Capital.

Benchmark Selection and Context

Benchmarks are presented alongside each portfolio's statistics:

Asymmetric Capital Gains Portfolio: Benchmarked against the MSCI ACWI index, reflecting its global diversification and opportunity set.

Diversified Income Portfolio: Benchmarked against an appropriate global high dividend yield index (e.g., MSCI ACWI High Dividend Yield Index), matched to the portfolio's dividend objective.

Benchmark sources, methodology, weighting, and limitations are disclosed in all performance material.

Reporting Periods and Updates

Performance tables display results over 1 year, 3 years, 5 years, and since inception, matching the long-term investment horizon of both strategies. Data is updated monthly for cumulative/compound returns, with annual figures refreshed each calendar year.

Risk Considerations

No explicit risk metrics (e.g., volatility, drawdown, Sharpe ratio) are provided. Strategy profiles indicate that the Asymmetric Gains approach may exhibit higher volatility due to its sector rotation and deep value focus, while the Diversified Income strategy is inherently lower risk due to disciplined position sizing and sustained dividend selection.

Placement and Disclosure Access

A summary disclosure accompanies each chart and table displaying performance, with the full disclosure statement accessible via the website footer and linked from all performance pages for detailed review.

Regulatory Compliance, Transparency, and Data Requests

Glenorchy Capital complies with GIPS and SEC requirements for performance presentation, with supporting calculation sheets, fee schedules, and data records available upon request by emailing admin@glenorchycapital.net. All assumptions, limitations, methodology choices, and material factors—including reliance on unaudited internal data—are fully explained for transparency.

Performance results are updated monthly, except year-end annual returns which are refreshed annually. We do not display YTD results, as our strategies are intended for long-term investment horizons.
