Chris MacIntosh

Co-founder / Principal

Chris MacIntosh is a co-founder and principal at Glenorchy Capital. With a career spanning investment banking at Lehman Brothers, JP Morgan, and Robert Fleming & Co., Chris has managed, built and sold multi-million dollar ventures across private & public equity, bonds, commodities, real estate and currencies. In addition to his hedge fund management, he previously served as CEO and CIO of Seraph Ventures, where he directed over $35 million into early-stage investments globally. His expertise encompasses venture capital, macroeconomic strategy, and rigorous investment research, which he shares through regular appearances as an industry thought leader.

Brad McFadden

Co-founder / Principal

Brad McFadden is a co-founder and principal at Glenorchy Capital, bringing over two decades of investment management experience internationally. Brad's pedigree covers proprietary trading at Rand Merchant Bank, portfolio management at Henry Ansbacher, and leadership roles in both institutional and independent asset management. Our Investment Philosophy

Contrarian by Nature

We actively seek opportunities where consensus is wrong. The best risk/reward comes from being early to recognize fundamental changes that others miss or dismiss.

Global Perspective

Opportunities don't respect borders. We invest wherever value exists—from Argentine equities to Japanese small-caps, from uranium miners to shipping companies.

Patient Capital

We're not traders. Our typical holding period is 3-5 years, allowing fundamental value to be recognized and compound. Short-term volatility is noise, not signal.

Partner With Experienced Managers

Join us in pursuing asymmetric opportunities in global markets. Our capital is invested alongside yours.
