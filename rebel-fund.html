Partnership with George Gammon

Created in collaboration with renowned entrepreneur and financial educator George Gammon, the Rebel Fund is designed for investors ready to position capital ahead of mainstream recognition.

Launched in 2024, the Rebel Fund seeks to multiply clients' capital by anticipating sectoral shifts and positioning in extremely out-of-favour opportunities.

Investment Strategy

Six core components drive our contrarian approach

Deep Value

Search global financial markets for extremely out-of-favour sectors trading at deep discounts.

Reducing Risk

Invest in multiple high-target return positions to reduce overall portfolio risk through diversification.

Non-Correlated

Target positions with little correlation to major market indices, providing genuine portfolio diversification.

Arbitraging Impatience

Leverage our long-term investment horizon against short-term market pressures and impatience.

Theme-Based

Simultaneously pursue up to a dozen sectors and themes, capitalizing on multiple opportunity sets.

Asymmetry

Target 3:1 or better risk-return ratios from themes and sectors with asymmetric upside potential.
